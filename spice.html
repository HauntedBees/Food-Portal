        Spiceapedia v1.0

        Hey hi welcome. This is a website where you can learn about spices, herbs, and spice blends (and counting!). You can find what seasonings go best with different foods and what dishes they're most commonly used in. It's a hoot.

        Tap that hamburgle menu item up top to bring down the search bar and filter options. If you know what spice you want to see, type its name in the search bar. Alternatively, you can filter by various criteria to find spices that pair well with certain foods or from certain regions.

        Click one of the spices on the left panel to see more information about that spice, or start typing into that search bar up there to find spices!

        You can automatically filter by some things by clickingtapping on highlighted words in information cards, such as region names, foods, and flavors. You can also do some advanced searching by typing things like foods:potato or dishes:marinade in the search bar!

        If you have any suggestions, corrections, additions, or comments, you can contact me via email or twitter! Have fun!
