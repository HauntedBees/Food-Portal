      AltFood Reviews v1.0

      Hey hi do you enjoy things like meat and dairy, but do not want to consume actual meat or dairy for whatever reason? Relatable! Unfortunately, a lot of meat and dairy substitutes kind of suck. Or at least, that's the common perception. So many fake meats, so few that actually taste or feel like meats!

      That's where this site comes in! I'm on a quest to try as many of them as I can so you don't have to, then I rate them based on how accurate they are in terms of flavor and texture, as well as if they taste good in general (sometimes they taste good even if they aren't super accurate)! I'm not a vegan or vegetarian, nor am I being paid by the vegan mafia, so I have no stakes in the game. I calls 'em like I sees 'em.

      If you have any comments, suggestions, criticisms, complaints, or recommendations, hit me up at fench@hauntedbees.com or on Twitter at @hauntedbees.
